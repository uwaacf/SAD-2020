Thanks to the help of Tom Nook, Timmy, and Tommy, Greedy Bell Michelle has been put behind bars. Eyewitness reports have all claimed that she has been going island to island and stealing from every villager in sight, all the while refusing to pay her debts to our beloved tanukis. When interrogated about her motives, Michelle simply replied that she needed the money for flowers for her own island. We sentence this burglar a fine of 100 Nook Miles.
